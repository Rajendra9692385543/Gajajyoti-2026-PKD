About GAJAJYOTI

Gajajyoti is the annual fest of Centurion University of Technology and Management, Paralakhemundi, celebrating the spirit of creativity, innovation, and culture.

The fest brings together students from all disciplines through exciting technical events, cultural shows, exhibitions, and competitions, showcasing Centurion’s vibrant energy and talent.

The grand highlight of the celebration is the Star Night, where renowned artists and performers light up the stage with music, dance, and entertainment—making GAJAJYOTI an unforgettable experience for everyone.
