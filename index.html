About GAJAJYOTI

Gajajyoti is the annual fest of Centurion University of Technology and Management, Paralakhemundi, celebrating the spirit of creativity, innovation, and culture.

The fest brings together students from all disciplines through exciting technical events, cultural shows, exhibitions, and competitions, showcasing Centurion‚Äôs vibrant energy and talent.

Message from the Registrar

I extend my warm greetings and best wishes to all the students, faculty members, organizers, and participants of Gajajyoti 2026, the annual techno, cultural, management, literary and skill fest of Centurion University of Technology and Management, Odisha, being organized from 19th February 2026 to 21st February 2026.

Gajajyoti stands as a vibrant celebration of holistic education, where academic excellence converges with innovation, creativity, leadership, and cultural diversity. The fest provides an enriching platform for students to showcase their talents, enhance their skills, and engage in meaningful exchanges that prepare them for professional and societal challenges.

I sincerely appreciate the dedication and hard work of the organizing committee, faculty mentors, and student volunteers whose collective efforts make this flagship event a reality. I am confident that Gajajyoti 2026 will foster collaboration, inspire young minds, and leave a lasting impact on all participants.

I wish the event great success and hope it continues to illuminate the spirit of excellence and innovation at Centurion University.

Best wishes,
Registrar

Odisha
